Story

In the near future, a great technological catastrophe has divided known reality into countless fragments, called "Zones". These Zones are parallel dimensions, each with their own laws of physics, ecosystems, societies, and dangers.

From neon-lit futuristic metropolises to fantastical realms where ancient magic reigns, to post-apocalyptic wastelands built upon the ruins of civilization...
